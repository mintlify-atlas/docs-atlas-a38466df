## Overview

The **Alerts Center** (Centro de Alertas y Oportunidades) is Visor KPI's proactive intelligence engine. Instead of manually searching for problems or opportunities, this view surfaces critical issues and growth potential automatically using 7 predefined business rules.

<Info>
  **Who uses this view**: Sales managers, commercial directors, and team leaders who need to stay ahead of issues before they impact results.
</Info>

## The 7 Alert Rules

Visor KPI evaluates these business rules automatically:

### ALT001: Seller Critical Underperformance

<Warning>
  **Trigger**: Seller with quota attainment < 65% in the last 30 days
  
  **Severity**: 🔴 Critical
  
  **Impact**: A seller this far below target is unlikely to recover without intervention
</Warning>

**What the alert shows**:
- Seller name and ID
- Current attainment percentage
- Gap to target
- Days remaining in period

**Suggested actions**:
- Review portfolio and objectives with supervisor
- Identify specific barriers (training, territory, resources)
- Create performance improvement plan
- Daily check-ins for remainder of month
- Consider territory reassignment if chronic

**Example**: 
```
🔴 [ALT001] · CRITICAL
Seller Critical Underperformance
Juan Pérez (North Zone) — 58% quota attainment (threshold: 65%)
💡 Review portfolio and objectives with supervisor
```

---

### ALT002: Client Inactivity Risk

<Warning>
  **Trigger**: Active client without purchases in the last 45 days
  
  **Severity**: 🟡 Medium
  
  **Impact**: Risk of permanent client loss, churn prevention opportunity
</Warning>

**What the alert shows**:
- Client name and ID
- Days since last purchase
- Historical purchase frequency
- Previous revenue contribution

**Suggested actions**:
- Urgent reactivation contact
- Understand reason for inactivity
- Offer special promotion or incentive
- Schedule in-person visit if high-value account

**Example**:
```
🟡 [ALT002] · MEDIUM
Client Inactivity Risk
Supermercado ABC — No purchases for 52 days
💡 Urgent reactivation contact
```

---

### ALT003: Key Account Revenue Drop

<Warning>
  **Trigger**: Category A client with sales decline > 25% vs previous month
  
  **Severity**: 🔴 Critical
  
  **Impact**: Your most valuable clients are buying significantly less
</Warning>

**What the alert shows**:
- Client name and Pareto classification
- % decline in purchases
- Dollar amounts (previous vs current)
- Trend direction

**Suggested actions**:
- Key account visit required
- Investigate root cause (competitive pressure, service issues, budget cuts)
- Prepare retention proposal
- Escalate to senior management if needed

**Example**:
```
🔴 [ALT003] · CRITICAL  
Key Account Revenue Drop
Distribuidora XYZ (Category A) — Down 32% ($85K → $58K)
💡 Key account visit required
```

---

### ALT004: Product Without Movement

<Info>
  **Trigger**: Active product without sales in 60 days
  
  **Severity**: 🔵 Low
  
  **Impact**: Inventory carrying costs, potential obsolescence
</Info>

**What the alert shows**:
- Product name and SKU code
- Days without sales
- Category
- Last sale date

**Suggested actions**:
- Review availability and stock levels
- Commercial push campaign
- Training for sales team
- Consider promotional pricing
- Evaluate discontinuation if chronic

**Example**:
```
🔵 [ALT004] · LOW
Product Without Movement  
Product XYZ (Beverages) — 73 days without sales
💡 Review availability and push commercial
```

---

### ALT005: Seller Inactivity

<Warning>
  **Trigger**: Seller without transactions in the last 7 business days
  
  **Severity**: 🟡 Medium
  
  **Impact**: Lost selling time, potential territory neglect
</Warning>

**What the alert shows**:
- Seller name
- Days since last activity
- Last transaction date
- Assigned territory

**Suggested actions**:
- Verify field activity immediately
- Check for illness, vacation, or administrative issues
- Review daily call reports
- Assess if support needed

**Example**:
```
🟡 [ALT005] · MEDIUM
Seller Inactivity
María González — No transactions in 9 business days
💡 Verify field activity
```

---

### ALT006: Growth Opportunity (Category B Client)

<Info>
  **Trigger**: Category B client with growth > 30% vs previous month
  
  **Severity**: 🟢 Opportunity
  
  **Impact**: Potential to upgrade to Category A (high-value client)
</Info>

**What the alert shows**:
- Client name and current category
- Growth percentage
- Revenue increase
- Purchase frequency change

**Suggested actions**:
- Upgrade to Category A status with increased visit frequency
- Offer volume discounts to lock in growth
- Expand product mix
- Assign senior account manager

**Example**:
```
🟢 [ALT006] · OPPORTUNITY
Growth Opportunity
Minimarket Del Sur (Category B) — +42% growth  
💡 Potential for upgrade to Category A — increase visit frequency
```

---

### ALT007: Team Near Target (Push Opportunity)

<Info>
  **Trigger**: Team cumulative attainment > 85% with 5+ business days remaining in month
  
  **Severity**: 🟢 Opportunity
  
  **Impact**: High probability of exceeding target with focused effort
</Info>

**What the alert shows**:
- Team attainment percentage
- Days remaining in period
- Gap to 100% target
- Required daily average to exceed

**Suggested actions**:
- Final push campaign for month close
- Daily performance meetings
- Small incentives for target achievement
- Focus on quick wins and closing pending deals

**Example**:
```
🟢 [ALT007] · OPPORTUNITY
Team Near Target
Commercial Team — 91% attainment | 7 business days remaining
💡 Final push for month close in green
```

## Alert Management

### Mark as Reviewed

Each alert card has an action button:

**✅ Mark as Reviewed**: Indicates you've seen and addressed this alert

**Why mark reviewed?**
- Prevents re-reviewing same alerts
- Tracks what's been handled vs what's pending
- Keeps your alert queue clean and actionable

<Note>
  Reviewed status persists only for your current session. On next login, new alerts will appear based on current data conditions.
</Note>

## Daily Alert Workflow

### Morning Routine (10 minutes)

**Every morning before planning your day**:

1. **Open Alerts Center**
2. **Check summary counts**
   - Any critical alerts?
   - How many medium priority?
3. **Filter to Critical**
4. **Review each critical alert**
5. **Add critical items to today's task list**
6. **Mark alerts as reviewed** as you address them

### Weekly Planning (30 minutes)

**Every Monday**:

1. **Review all Medium priority alerts**
2. **Categorize by theme**:
   - Client issues
   - Seller performance  
   - Product problems
3. **Assign to appropriate team members**
4. **Set deadlines for resolution**
5. **Schedule follow-up review for Friday**

### Monthly Review (1 hour)

**End of each month**:

1. **Export all alerts from past 30 days**
2. **Analyze patterns**:
   - Which alerts recur?
   - Which sellers/clients/products appear most?
   - Are we improving or declining?
3. **Update processes** to prevent recurring issues
4. **Adjust thresholds** if too many false positives
5. **Share insights** with leadership team

## Alert Effectiveness Metrics

Track how well you're responding to alerts:

### Response Rate
```
(Alerts Marked Reviewed / Total Alerts Generated) × 100
Target: > 90%
```

### Average Response Time
```
Average days from alert generation to review
Target: < 2 days for Critical, < 7 days for Medium
```

### Resolution Rate
```
(Alerts that don't recur / Total Alerts) × 100
Target: > 75%
```

<Note>
  If the same alert keeps appearing for the same entity, you're treating symptoms not causes. Dig deeper to resolve root issues.
</Note>

## Customization Options

### Threshold Adjustment

While the 7 alert rules are preconfigured, thresholds can be adjusted in `config.py` if needed:

- ALT001: Default 65% → Adjust for your market
- ALT002: Default 45 days → Adjust for purchase cycles
- ALT003: Default -25% → Adjust for volatility
- ALT006: Default +30% → Adjust for growth expectations

<Tip>
  Start with defaults for 3 months, then adjust based on false positive rates. Aim for a signal-to-noise ratio where 80%+ of alerts require actual action.
</Tip>
