## What Problem Does It Solve?

Many distribution companies still manage their sales data with multiple Excel files, manual reports, and hours invested consolidating scattered information. **When data is fragmented, making good business decisions becomes slow and difficult.**

Visor KPI Comercial solves this by:

- **Eliminating manual analysis** — converts Excel sales files into automatically processed KPIs
- **Centralizing sales intelligence** — managers and salespeople access their metrics from a single dashboard
- **Anticipating problems** — an alert engine detects performance drops, at-risk clients, and growth opportunities before they impact results
- **Democratizing data** — each salesperson sees their individual report; management gets consolidated team views

## What You Get

The system processes **27 months of historical data**, tracking:

- **12 salespeople** with performance profiles (star, stable, developing, problematic)
- **180 clients** across 4 commercial channels
- **60 SKUs** in 5 product categories
- **~30,000 transactions** with automated validation
- **7 active alert rules** (ALT001–ALT007)
- **Less than 3 second load time** with full dataset

## Technical Stack

Built with modern Python data tools for performance and reliability:

- **Streamlit** 1.32 — Multi-page interactive dashboard framework
- **Plotly** 5.19 — Rich interactive visualizations (charts, treemaps, gauges)
- **Pandas** 2.2 — ETL pipeline and data analysis
- **NumPy** 1.26 — Statistical calculations and numeric processing
