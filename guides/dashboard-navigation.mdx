## Dashboard Structure

Visor KPI Comercial is organized into **six main views**, each designed for specific analytical needs:

```
📊 Visor KPI Comercial
├── 🏠 Resumen Ejecutivo (Home)
├── 📈 1. Gerencia
├── 👤 2. Vendedores
├── 🏢 3. Clientes
├── 📦 4. Productos
└── 🚨 5. Alertas
```

## Vendedores

**Purpose**: Individual salesperson performance tracking

**Location**: `pages/2_vendedores.py`

**Features**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ranking Table">
    Sortable table showing all salespeople with:
    - Current rank and rank change (🔺🔻)
    - Sales amount and target achievement %
    - Traffic light indicator (🟢🟡🔴)
    - Unique customers served
    - Average ticket size
  </Step>

  <Step title="Individual Drill-Down">
    Click any salesperson to see:
    - Monthly sales evolution vs. target
    - Customer portfolio (top customers)
    - Product mix
    - Activity timeline
  </Step>

  <Step title="Comparison Tools">
    Compare multiple salespeople side-by-side:
    - Performance trends
    - Territory coverage
    - Customer acquisition
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Key Metrics**:
- `importe_total` - Total sales for period
- `cumplimiento_pct` - Target achievement (from `kpi_engine.py:171-173`)
- `ticket_promedio` - Average transaction per customer
- `ranking_actual` - Current rank position
- `delta_ranking` - Position change vs. previous period

## Clientes

**Purpose**: Customer portfolio analysis and retention

**Location**: `pages/3_clientes.py`

**Features**:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pareto Analysis" icon="chart-pie">
    Automatic ABC classification:
    - **Category A**: Top 20% customers (80% of revenue)
    - **Category B**: Next 30% customers (15% of revenue)
    - **Category C**: Remaining 50% customers (5% of revenue)
  </Card>

  <Card title="Risk Flags" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Identifies at-risk customers:
    - Category A/B with >30 days no purchase
    - Declining purchase patterns
    - Activity alerts
  </Card>

  <Card title="Customer Details" icon="address-card">
    Drill-down shows:
    - Purchase history and frequency
    - Assigned salesperson
    - Channel and zone
    - Last purchase date
  </Card>

  <Card title="Retention Metrics" icon="chart-area">
    Track customer health:
    - New customers acquired
    - Lost customers (previous buyers, now inactive)
    - Recovered customers
    - Coverage percentage
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Technical Note**:
Risk flag logic is defined in `kpi_engine.py:315-318`:
```python
flag_en_riesgo = (
    (categoria_pareto in ["A", "B"]) &
    (dias_desde_ultima_compra > 30)
)
```

## Productos

**Purpose**: Product performance and category analysis

**Location**: `pages/4_productos.py`

**Features**:

<Accordion title="Product Rankings">
  Sortable table with:
  - Revenue by product
  - Units sold
  - Transaction count
  - Pareto category (A/B/C)
  - Period-over-period change
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Category Analysis">
  - Sales distribution by category
  - Category growth trends
  - Mix evolution over time
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Slow Movers">
  Identifies products with:
  - No sales in 60+ days (Alert ALT004)
  - Declining trend
  - Low rotation
</Accordion>

**Pareto Calculation**:
Implemented in `src/pareto.py`, automatically applied in `kpi_engine.py:240`:
- Sorts products by revenue descending
- Calculates cumulative percentage
- Assigns A/B/C categories based on 80/15/5 rule

## Alertas

**Purpose**: Centralized alert management and action tracking

**Location**: `pages/5_alertas.py`

**Alert Types** (7 rules defined in `alerts_engine.py:14-71`):

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="ALT001: Vendor Low Performance" icon="user-xmark" color="#E84855">
    **Severity**: 🔴 Critical
    
    **Condition**: Salesperson with &lt;65% target achievement in last 30 days
    
    **Action**: Review portfolio and targets with supervisor
  </Card>

  <Card title="ALT002: Customer No Purchases" icon="building" color="#FFB347">
    **Severity**: 🟡 Medium
    
    **Condition**: Active customer with no purchases in 45+ days
    
    **Action**: Urgent recovery contact
  </Card>

  <Card title="ALT003: Category A Drop" icon="chart-line-down" color="#E84855">
    **Severity**: 🔴 Critical
    
    **Condition**: Category A customer with >25% revenue drop vs. last month
    
    **Action**: Key account visit
  </Card>

  <Card title="ALT004: Product No Movement" icon="box" color="#4A90E2">
    **Severity**: 🔵 Low
    
    **Condition**: Active product with no sales in 60+ days
    
    **Action**: Review availability and commercial push
  </Card>

  <Card title="ALT005: Vendor Inactive" icon="user-clock" color="#FFB347">
    **Severity**: 🟡 Medium
    
    **Condition**: No transactions in last 7 business days
    
    **Action**: Verify field activity
  </Card>

  <Card title="ALT006: Category B Growth" icon="arrow-trend-up" color="#00C49F">
    **Severity**: 🟢 Opportunity
    
    **Condition**: Category B customer with >30% growth
    
    **Action**: Potential upgrade to A - increase visit frequency
  </Card>

  <Card title="ALT007: Team Near Target" icon="bullseye" color="#00C49F">
    **Severity**: 🟢 Opportunity
    
    **Condition**: Team >85% achievement with 5+ business days remaining
    
    **Action**: Final push to close month in green
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Alert Management**:
- Filter by severity, entity type
- Mark alerts as "Reviewed" (session-based)
- Toggle to hide reviewed alerts
- Alert counter by severity level

<Tip>
Alerts are recalculated in real-time based on your filter selections. Use the date range and zone filters to focus on specific areas.
</Tip>

## Global Sidebar

The sidebar is consistent across all pages and provides:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Logo & Title">
    **📊 Visor KPI**
    
    Performance Comercial
  </Step>

  <Step title="Period Filter" icon="calendar">
    Preset options:
    - Hoy (Today)
    - Esta semana (This week)
    - Este mes (This month)
    - Último mes (Last month)
    - Este trimestre (This quarter)
    - Último trimestre (Last quarter)
    - Este año (This year)
    - Último año (Last year)
    - **Personalizado** (Custom range)
    
    <Note>
    Custom range shows two date pickers for "Desde" and "Hasta" (From/To)
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Zone Filter" icon="map">
    Multi-select for sales zones (populated from `Vendedores.zona`)
    
    Typical values: Norte, Sur, Centro, CABA, GBA
  </Step>

  <Step title="Salesperson Filter" icon="user">
    Multi-select showing active salespeople:
    - Format: "Nombre Completo (Zona)"
    - Dynamically filtered by selected zones
    - Only shows active (`activo = TRUE`) salespeople
  </Step>

  <Step title="Channel Filter" icon="store">
    Multi-select for customer channels (from `Clientes.canal`)
    
    Typical values: Retail, Distribuidores, E-commerce, Mayorista
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clear Filters Button" icon="rotate">
    🔄 **Limpiar filtros**
    
    Resets all filters to default state (current month, all zones/vendors/channels)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Last Update Indicator" icon="clock">
    Shows current date as reference for data freshness
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigation Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Drill-Down Workflow" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Recommended flow:
    1. Start at **Resumen Ejecutivo** for overview
    2. Identify areas needing attention (alerts, low performers)
    3. Navigate to specific page (**Vendedores**, **Clientes**, etc.)
    4. Apply filters to isolate issue
    5. Check **Alertas** for recommended actions
  </Card>

  <Card title="Filter Persistence" icon="floppy-disk">
    Filters are stored in `st.session_state` and persist as you navigate between pages.
    
    <Tip>
    Use this to compare metrics across different views with consistent filters.
    </Tip>
  </Card>

  <Card title="Keyboard Shortcuts" icon="keyboard">
    Streamlit default shortcuts:
    - `R` - Rerun the app
    - `Ctrl/Cmd + K` - Open command palette
    - `C` - Clear cache
  </Card>

  <Card title="Mobile View" icon="mobile">
    The dashboard is responsive but optimized for desktop/tablet.
    
    For mobile: Focus on summary cards and rankings. Detailed charts work best on larger screens.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Page Load Performance

<Note>
**First Load**: 2-5 seconds (builds cache)

**Subsequent Loads**: &lt;1 second (uses cached data)

**Cache Duration**: 1 hour
</Note>

All data loading functions use `@st.cache_data` with `ttl=3600` for optimal performance across pages.
