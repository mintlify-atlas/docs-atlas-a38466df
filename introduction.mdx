## Welcome to Visor KPI Comercial

Visor KPI Comercial is a sales intelligence dashboard that transforms raw Excel sales data into actionable business insights. Designed for distribution companies, it eliminates manual analysis, centralizes commercial intelligence, and enables data-driven decision making.

<Note>
  **Live Demo Available**: Experience the full interactive application at [https://excel-to-kpi-dashboard.streamlit.app/](https://excel-to-kpi-dashboard.streamlit.app/)
</Note>

## The Problem It Solves

Many distribution companies still manage their commercial information with multiple Excel files, manual reports, and hours of work invested in consolidating data that arrives late, scattered, and with errors.

The real problem isn't just operational effort: **when data is dispersed, making good business decisions becomes difficult and slow.**

**Visor KPI Comercial solves exactly that:**

## Key Business Questions Answered

This system is designed to answer the questions that matter most in a sales team:

- Which salespeople are exceeding or falling short of their targets?
- Which customers concentrate most of the revenue?
- Which products are really driving the business?
- Where are the opportunities or risks in the commercial portfolio?
- Which customers are at risk of stopping purchases?
- Which alerts require immediate action from the team?

## System Overview

Visor KPI Comercial provides **five integrated views** for comprehensive sales intelligence:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Executive Summary">
    KPIs of the business on a single screen — total sales, target fulfillment, gross margin, and customer coverage, with rankings of top sellers, customers, and products for the period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Management View">
    Monthly evolution of the complete team, performance comparison between sellers, and ranking with variation vs. previous period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seller View">
    Each salesperson accesses their own dashboard with quota fulfillment, team ranking position, personal sales evolution, and portfolio details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Analysis">
    Pareto A/B/C segmentation of the portfolio, frequency vs. amount scatter plot, Gini concentration index, and identification of customers at risk of inactivity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Product Analysis">
    Portfolio treemap by category and volume, Pareto of products, identification of SKUs without movement, and trend analysis by product.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Alerts & Opportunities Center">
    Unified hub with active alerts classified by severity (critical, medium, low) and automatically detected opportunities, with suggested actions for each case.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Business Intelligence Capabilities

### Automated ETL

<Card icon="database">
  - **Excel ingestion** without manual configuration: the system reads, validates, and transforms the sales file into a reproducible pipeline with `pandas` + `openpyxl`
  - Automatic detection of nulls, inconsistent dates, and outliers
  - Data normalization by channel, zone, and period
</Card>

### KPI Engine

Automated calculation of the most critical commercial indicators:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Period Sales | Total revenue and by segment |
| Gross Margin | Profitability by seller, customer, and product |
| Quota Fulfillment | % of target reached vs monthly goal |
| Portfolio Coverage | % of active customers over total assigned |
| Variation vs Previous Period | Absolute and relative delta MoM / YoY |
| Purchase Frequency | Recurrence by customer and channel |
| Product Rotation | Active vs inactive SKUs by period |

### Pareto Analysis 80/20

<Card icon="chart-pie">
  - Automatic classification of customers and products in **A / B / C** categories
  - Calculation of the **Gini concentration index** of the portfolio
  - Identification of customers generating 80% of revenue
  - Detection of low-rotation SKUs with potential stockout risk
</Card>

### Intelligent Alert Engine

7 pre-configured business rules that trigger real-time alerts:

| ID | Alert | Severity |
|----|-------|----------|
| ALT001 | Seller with fulfillment < 70% | Critical |
| ALT002 | Customer without purchase in last 30 days | High |
| ALT003 | Product with sales drop > 30% MoM | High |
| ALT004 | Customer portfolio with excessive concentration (Gini > 0.6) | Medium |
| ALT005 | Margin below minimum threshold | Critical |
| ALT006 | Portfolio coverage < 60% | High |
| ALT007 | Opportunity: customer with high frequency and low ticket | Opportunity |

## Project Architecture

The project follows a clean architecture with clear separation of concerns:

```
visor_kpi/
├── app.py                     # Executive Summary (main page)
├── config.py                  # Colors, thresholds, formats
├── requirements.txt
│
├── pages/                     # Multi-page views
│   ├── 1_gerencia.py          # Consolidated management view
│   ├── 2_vendedores.py        # Individual seller view
│   ├── 3_clientes.py          # Customer portfolio analysis
│   ├── 4_productos.py         # Product ranking and analysis
│   └── 5_alertas.py           # Alerts and opportunities center
│
├── src/                       # Business logic core
│   ├── data_loader.py         # ETL: reading, validation, and Excel filtering
│   ├── kpi_engine.py          # KPI calculation engine
│   ├── pareto.py              # 80/20 analysis and A/B/C classification
│   ├── alerts_engine.py       # Alert engine (ALT001–ALT007)
│   └── reports.py             # Exportable report generation
│
├── components/                # Reusable UI
│   ├── kpi_card.py            # KPI cards with traffic light
│   ├── charts.py              # Plotly visualizations
│   ├── filters.py             # Sidebar filters with session_state
│   └── rankings.py            # Ranking tables with styled HTML
│
├── data/
│   ├── mock/
│   │   └── generate_mock_data.py   # Demo data generator (Faker es_AR)
│   └── raw/
│       └── mock_data.xlsx          # Generated locally
│
└── tests/
    ├── test_mock_data.py       # Dataset validation
    ├── test_kpis.py            # KPI engine tests
    └── test_alerts.py          # Alert engine tests
```

<Note>
  **Clear layer separation:**
  - `src/` → Pure business logic, 100% testable and UI-independent
  - `components/` → Reusable presentation layer
  - `pages/` → Streamlit multi-page view orchestration
</Note>
