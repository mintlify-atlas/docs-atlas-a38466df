## Overview

The **Executive Summary** is the main landing page of Visor KPI Comercial, providing leadership with a comprehensive view of business performance in seconds. This dashboard consolidates the most critical commercial KPIs, team performance rankings, and active alerts into a single, actionable view.

<Info>
  **Who uses this view**: C-level executives, commercial directors, and management who need to understand business health at a glance without drilling into detailed reports.
</Info>

## Key Metrics Displayed

### Primary KPI Cards

Four prominent metrics are displayed at the top of the dashboard:

<CardGroup cols={2}>

    - Comparison vs previous period (% change)
    - Progress toward monthly/quarterly target
    - Visual indicator (green/orange/red)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Team Performance" icon="bullseye">
    Overall team quota attainment:
    - Percentage of target achieved
    - Target: 100%
    - Color-coded status indicator
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Gross Margin" icon="chart-line">
    Total gross margin for the period:
    - Absolute margin value in currency
    - % change vs previous period
    - Profitability health indicator
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Client Coverage" icon="users">
    Percentage of active clients with purchases:
    - Current coverage percentage
    - Visual health indicator
    - Portfolio activation metric
  </Card>

### Performance Gauge

The **Team Performance Gauge** provides instant visual feedback on quota attainment:

- **Green zone (100%+)**: Team exceeding target
- **Orange zone (80-99%)**: Team approaching target
- **Red zone (&lt;80%)**: Team below expectations

Below the gauge, you'll see a breakdown of your sales team:
- 🟢 **In Green**: Number of sellers exceeding quota
- 🟡 **In Orange**: Number of sellers approaching quota
- 🔴 **In Red**: Number of sellers underperforming

## Sales Evolution Chart

The **Monthly Sales Evolution** chart shows:

- Historical sales trend (up to 13 months)
- Actual sales vs targets for each month
- Interactive hover details with exact values
- Trend identification for forecasting

<Note>
  The evolution chart automatically adjusts to your selected date range in the sidebar filters.
</Note>

## Filters and Navigation

Use the **sidebar filters** to adjust your view:

- **Date Range**: Select period (default: current month)
- **Sellers**: Filter by specific team members
- **Zones**: Filter by geographic territory
- **Channels**: Focus on specific sales channels

<Tip>
  Changes to filters automatically recalculate all KPIs and refresh all visualizations in real-time.
</Tip>

## Workflow Example

**Monday Morning Executive Review**:

1. Open Executive Summary
2. Check main KPI cards for period status
3. Review performance gauge for team health
4. Scan active alerts for critical issues
5. Identify top performers and potential problems
6. Click through to detailed views as needed

**Total time**: 2-3 minutes for complete business overview

## Data Calculations

All metrics are calculated from the transaction dataset:

- **Sales Total**: Sum of `importe_neto` for filtered period
- **% vs Previous**: Comparison against equivalent prior period
- **Quota Attainment**: Actual sales / assigned target
- **Gross Margin**: Sum of `margen_neto` / sum of `importe_neto`
- **Coverage**: Unique clients with purchases / total active clients

<Note>
  The system automatically handles period comparisons by calculating the equivalent date range in the prior period (e.g., if viewing Jan 1-15, 2024, the comparison is Jan 1-15, 2023).
</Note>
